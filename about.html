About Me

Meet the creator!

Hi! My name is Catalina, but most call me by "Cat." Originally from Orange County, CA, I moved to Honolulu, HI to attend high school there. Now, I am an undergraduate attenting Loyola Marymount University as a Computer Science major and Psychology minor. I enjoy various activites: from the adrenaline-spiking ones like surfing and skating, creative ones like ceramics, graphic design and writing, to relaxing ones like playing video games, practicing guitar, taking walks and finding new recipes to cook. I am always on the lookout to pick up new skills! Even though coding is quite new to me, I have learned a lot through help from classes, peers and online tutorials. I am motivated to gain the skills to one day craft my ideas into reality. As someone with Generalized Anxiety Disorder, my main goal is to combine the fundamentals of technology and psychology to create something which can easily provide care for those suffering from mild to severe anxiety symptoms. Especially those who are unable to seek out immediate mental health treatment. That is the purpose of the start of "catCreations." Hopefully, I will be able to extend this technology to ease the symptoms of those suffering from other various mental health conditions.
