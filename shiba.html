Purpose:

I have always had a passion for supporting those who struggle with mental health, I realized I could aid those suffering from anxiety on a greater scale. I want to build an app that focuses on tackling anxiety symptoms before they spiral out of control. My goal for the app is to make sure that those affected by anxiety, especially those who are younger, would never have to suffer through the symptoms of a panic attack alone again. I began to plan each of the steps I need to take to make my goal a reality. I searched up tutorials on YouTube and found coding tools which I would then use to expand my knowledge in programming languages such as Swift. I also want to make this app feel safe for people with anxiety disorders; in fact, I based the app mascot off of my Shiba-Inu plushie because I sought comfort from this giant plushie since middle school. I figured my Shiba-Inu plushie could bring that same comfort to others suffering from anxiety as well
